# Exponential Probability Distribution

The Exponential [[Probability Distribution\|Probability Distribution]] is one of the most used [[Continuous Probability Distribution\|Continuous Probability Distribution]], often used to model the time elapsed between [[Event\|Events]]. 

It's considered a particular case of the [[Gamma Probability Distribution\|Gamma Probability Distribution]]
## Formulas

> [!formula] [[Probability Density Function\|Probability Density Function]] of the Exponential Distribution
> #formula $$f(x) = \lambda e^{\lambda x}$$

> [!formula] [[Cumulative Distribution Function\|Cumulative Distribution Function]] of the Exponential Distribution
> #formula $$F(x) = P(X \leq x) = 1 - e^{-\lambda x}$$

## Properties

> [!formula] [[01-atomic/math/Expected Value\|Expected Value]] of the Exponential Distribution
> #formula $$E[X] = \frac{1}{\lambda}$$

> [!formula] [[01-atomic/math/Variance\|Variance]] of the Exponential Distribution
> #formula $$Var(X) = \frac{1}{\lambda^2}$$

The Exponential Probability Distribution is [[Memoryless\|Memoryless]]
